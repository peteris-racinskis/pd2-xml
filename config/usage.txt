Usage: ./main.py [COMMAND] [ARGS]
	Commands:
	-   update [FILE][, [FILE], ... ] - update database;
	-   display [OPTION] - display statistics;
	Display options:
	top     - show top 10 teams by score;
	player  - show top 10 players by goals;
	rough   - show players by penalty count;
	referee - show referee stats.
